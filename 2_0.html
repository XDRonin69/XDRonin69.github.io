About college

Sri Ramakrishna College of Arts and Science [Formerly SNR Sons College] is one among the most renowned Arts and Science Educational Intuitions where excellence is tradition. It is affiliated to Bharathiar University and accredited by National Assessment and Academic Council with Grade “A+” in its 4th cycle. The college enjoys autonomous status since 2004. It is an ISO 9001:2015 certified college and the first Arts and Science College in the region to receive International Accreditation with “A” grade from the CIAC. The college is under STAR College scheme of the Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Government of India since 2019. SRCAS holds a partnership with the Institution Innovation Council and some of our academic partners are Amity Global Institute-Singapore, Asia Pacific University-Malaysia, Cyber Jaya University-Malaysia, Sunway University-Malaysia, Lahti University of Applied Science-Finland, and Association of Chartered Certified Accountants. Our College has ranked 84th in NIRF 2021.

About Department

PG and Research Department of Computer Science was established in the year 1987. It has well experienced, qualified, committed faculty members who produce excellent results and good research publications. The Department is supported by well equipped air-conditioned Computer Laboratories with latest configuration systems and internet facilities. The students are taught the curriculum with interpersonal and intrapersonal skills. They are also trained for their placements and it paves the way to be an entrepreneur. Our students are motivated and moulded to face the modern competitive technological environment to compete professionally and proficiently. The department is equipped with quality resources for imparting the needs and carrier based education using contemporary teaching techniques. The department offers following programmes B.Sc., M.Sc., M.Phil., and Ph.D.
